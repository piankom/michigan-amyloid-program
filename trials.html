Active Clinical Trials

The Michigan Amyloid Program offers access to cutting-edge clinical trials for patients with amyloidosis. Below are some current opportunities actively enrolling at Michigan Medicine:

Phase 2 Study of Investigational Drug B in AL Amyloidosis

Condition: Systemic AL Amyloidosis

This phase 2 trial investigates the safety and response rates of Drug B in patients with relapsed or refractory AL amyloidosis. Includes MRD monitoring and biomarker analysis.

Status: Recruiting

View on ClinicalTrials.gov
Natural History Registry for Transthyretin (ATTR) Amyloidosis

Condition: Hereditary and Wild-Type ATTR

A non-interventional study collecting long-term outcomes and treatment history in patients with confirmed ATTR amyloidosis. Includes periodic cardiac and neurologic assessments.

Status: Enrolling by Invitation

View on ClinicalTrials.gov
Multi-Arm Platform Trial for Amyloid Neuropathy

Condition: Amyloid Peripheral Neuropathy

This adaptive study compares multiple agents targeting nerve involvement in systemic amyloidosis. Open to patients with confirmed nerve-predominant disease or autonomic symptoms.

Status: Not Yet Recruiting

View on ClinicalTrials.gov
